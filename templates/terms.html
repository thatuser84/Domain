1. what this is

characterground is a fiction roleplay tool. It generates text via a third-party language model (currently served through Groq, using an API key you provide yourself) based on character sheets and prompts you write. Everything produced — dialogue, narration, scenarios — is fictional. No part of it reflects the views, consent, or involvement of any real person unless you are the one writing it. Accounts are individual — each person brings their own model access key, and each person's conversations are private to them.

2. who can use this

You must be at least 18 years old to use this app. By using it, you're confirming you meet that requirement and that you're accessing it in a jurisdiction where doing so is legal.

3. moderation is narrow, not a general filter

This app runs automated checks for a specific, narrow set of things: sexual content involving minors, non-consensual sexualization of a real identifiable person, and genuinely actionable illegal real-world content. Content flagged in those categories can be blocked before it's created or sent. Everything else — explicit sexual content, violence, taboo or dark themes, fictional non-consent between adult characters — is intentionally left alone; the "tone dial" on each character is a flavor setting, not a safety system, and ordinary adult fiction is the entire point of this app. Automated checks can miss things or make mistakes in either direction; they're a backstop, not a guarantee, and responsibility for what you write and read still rests with you.

4. chats are not private from review

Conversations are stored in this app's database, tied to your account. Stored chats can be reviewed by a human (the operator of this instance) — for debugging, maintenance, abuse investigation, or legal compliance. Don't treat anything written here as unreadable by anyone but you.

5. public characters & the community page

You can mark a character you create as "public." Doing so shares that character's sheet — name, persona, scenario, tags — on the community page for other accounts to discover and start their own conversations with. It does not share your conversations: every account that chats with a public character gets their own private thread, invisible to the character's creator and to everyone else. Public character sheets go through the same moderation described above before they're published, and can still be taken down afterward — by you, or by the operator — if they're found to violate these terms.

6. enforcement

Content that crosses from fiction into something that targets a real, identifiable person without consent, depicts minors in a sexual context, or otherwise constitutes illegal content, is not covered by "it's just fiction" and is not tolerated. If material like that is found — whether by the automated checks, on review, or reported by someone else — it can result in the content being removed, your access being suspended or permanently banned, and — where required by law — reported to the relevant authorities or pursued through legal action.

7. no warranty

This is a personal project, not a commercial product. It's provided as-is, with no guarantee of uptime, accuracy, or fitness for any particular purpose. Model output can be unpredictable — treat everything it generates as exactly that: generated, not factual, not advice.

8. changes

These terms can change as the app changes. Continuing to use characterground after an update means you accept the current version. You explicitly accepted these terms when you signed up; material changes may ask you to accept again.
