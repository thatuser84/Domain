1. what this is

characterground is a private, single-operator fiction roleplay tool. It generates text via a third-party language model (currently served through Groq) based on character sheets and prompts you write. Everything produced — dialogue, narration, scenarios — is fictional. No part of it reflects the views, consent, or involvement of any real person unless you are the one writing it.

2. who can use this

You must be at least 18 years old to use this app. By using it, you're confirming you meet that requirement and that you're accessing it in a jurisdiction where doing so is legal.

3. no content safeguards

This app does not run automated content moderation, age-verification on generated text, or filtering on what characters can say or do. The "tone dial" on each character is a flavor setting, not a safety system. There are no guardrails beyond your own judgment about what you generate. Use of this tool, and responsibility for the content you prompt and read, rests entirely with you.

4. chats are not private from review

Conversations are stored in this app's database, tied to your account. Despite the lack of automated moderation, stored chats can still be reviewed by a human (the operator of this instance) — for debugging, maintenance, abuse investigation, or legal compliance. Don't treat anything written here as unreadable by anyone but you.

5. enforcement

Content that crosses from fiction into something that targets a real, identifiable person without consent, depicts minors in a sexual context, or otherwise constitutes illegal content, is not covered by "it's just fiction" and is not tolerated. If material like that is found on review, it can result in your access being suspended or permanently banned, and — where required by law — reported to the relevant authorities or pursued through legal action.

6. no warranty

This is a personal project, not a commercial product. It's provided as-is, with no guarantee of uptime, accuracy, or fitness for any particular purpose. Model output can be unpredictable — treat everything it generates as exactly that: generated, not factual, not advice.

7. changes

These terms can change as the app changes. Continuing to use characterground after an update means you accept the current version.
